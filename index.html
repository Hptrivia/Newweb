How it works

Trivia Gauntlet is a browser-based trivia experience with a wide range of quiz themes and challenge modes. Pick a category, start playing instantly, and answer questions across different difficulty levels. The game is designed for quick solo play, friendly competition, and repeat sessions when you want a new theme or a tougher challenge.

Players can jump into quizzes based on popular series, blockbuster movies, well-known video games, football, basketball, combat sports, and general entertainment topics. New themes can be added over time, so the content library keeps growing instead of staying static.

What you can play

The game includes trivia for fans of long-running TV comedies, prestige dramas, fantasy series, action franchises, major game releases, and competitive sports. Whether you want something casual or difficult, the aim is to give players a simple way to jump between fandoms and test how much they really remember.

This site is intended for players who enjoy quiz nights, fandom knowledge battles, and quick challenges they can return to regularly. It works directly in the browser, so there is no separate app store download needed to start playing.

Note: if the game takes a moment to load, that is because the interactive app is being served live. Once it opens, you can start playing immediately inside the page.
